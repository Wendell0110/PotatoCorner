INTERNATIONAL FRANCHISING



Welcome to the Potato Corner International Franchise Opportunity!


Are you ready to embark on a flavorful journey with Potato Corner, the world’s best flavored french fries? We are actively seeking passionate individuals or

groups to join our family of successful franchisees. Here’s what it takes to be part of the Potato Corner success story:


The 4-C’s of Potato Corner Franchise Selection:


      1. Capability:

  •  We value experience! Ideal candidates have a background in F&B operations, retail, or hospitality.

  •  Demonstrated ability to manage and expand multi-store operations is a significant advantage.

      2. Capital:

  •  Financial strength is key! Franchisees must possess sufficient funds to meet the development commitment.

  •  Supported by strong financials, ensuring a stable foundation for success.

       3. Commitment:

  •  Ready for a long-term partnership! Candidates should be willing to sign a 20-store development franchise agreement.

  •  Willingness to invest in building a strong team is essential for brand growth.

      4. Culture:

  •  Embrace growth! We are looking for individuals with a growth-oriented mindset.

  •  Customer-centric approach – believe in creating exceptional experiences.

  •  Firm belief in the Potato Corner brand and seamless alignment with SPAVI WOW Culture.

Territories Open for Franchise Applications:

Potato Corner is actively seeking franchisees in these exciting territories:

  •  Indonesia

  •  Vietnam

  •  Hong Kong

  •  Taiwan

  •  Korea

  •  Japan

  •  New Zealand

  •  United Kingdom

  •  Canada (except British Columbia, Alberta, Manitoba, Ontario)

  •  Australia

If your desired location is not currently listed, we apologize. Rest assured, we will retain your application records on file. When the opportunity arises for

expansion into your preferred territory, we will promptly reach out to you.


Join us in spreading the joy of Potato Corner worldwide. Contact us today to explore this exciting franchise opportunity!
