FRANCHISING







APPLICATION PROCESS & REQUIREMENTS

FRANCHISING POTATO CORNER IS FOR YOU! TO GUIDE YOU, THE FOLLOWING GIVES A GENERAL OVERVIEW ON
HOW TO START YOUR OWN POTATO CORNER BUSINESS:

STEP 1: REQUIREMENTS

       a. Accomplished Letter of Intent

       b. 2 Valid IDs (SSS, Voters, Postal, Passport, Philhealth, Pag-ibig, UMID)

       c. Accomplished Application Form

       d. Accomplished Market Study and Site Analysis Form (with actual photos and measurements of the area)

       e. Draft lease Offer/Contract from the Lessor if renting


STEP 2 : INITIAL INTERVIEW

STEP 3: SITE INSPECTION AND APPROVAL (2-3 WEEKS)

STEP 4: GRANT MEETING (AWARDING OF SITE)

STEP 5: CONTRACT SIGNING AND PAYMENT

STEP 6: STORE CONSTRUCTION OR PREPARATION OF STORE OPENING

STEP 7: STORE OPENING!


Our Team will acknowledge your submission through email within 1-2 business days, and the approval process may take a maximum of 1 month.

You may also call us for a follow-up at 0917-714-2083 or email us at iwantfranchise@potatocorner.com. Thank you!
