e. in the event Recipient is legally required to disclose any portion of the Confidential Information, Recipient shall promptly notify the Discloser so that the Discloser may take steps to protect its Confidential Information.
5. It is agreed that the confidentiality and non-use commitments set forth in paragraph 4 above shall not extend to any portion of Confidential Information which is known to the
